Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to open an account?

You'll need a valid government-issued ID, proof of address (utility bill or lease agreement), and Social Security number. For business accounts, additional documentation such as business license and tax ID may be required.

How can I access my account online?

You can access your account through our secure online banking platform or mobile app. Simply register with your account number and follow the setup process. You'll receive login credentials via secure email.

What are your loan interest rates?

Our loan interest rates vary based on the type of loan, credit score, and current market conditions. Personal loans start from 5.99% APR, while mortgage rates are currently starting from 3.25% APR. Contact us for personalized rates.

Is my money insured with SecureBank?

Yes, all deposits are insured by the FDIC up to $250,000 per depositor, per insured bank. This means your money is protected even in the unlikely event of bank failure.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support is available 24/7 through phone at 1-800-SECURE-BANK, live chat on our website, or email at support@securebank.com. You can also visit any of our branch locations during business hours.
